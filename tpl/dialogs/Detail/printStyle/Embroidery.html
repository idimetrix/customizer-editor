Embroidery is one of the most beautiful and high quality textile decoration methods. We offer embroidery even for a single piece of product. On our webpage are embroidery products marked with a special logo. It is possible to use some of our motives to be embroidered but the best result is achieved by using text, with broad selection of fonts and colors. It is not possible to keep high quality results on too big or very small letters.

Text and motives are prepared on computer. Embroidery machine uses more needles to precisely embroider the prepared graphics to the garment.

The results are full rich colors thanks to high quality threads. Along with the fabric or garment to be sewn is hooped special material as a backing. This is needed to stabilize the stitches and acquire crispness of the result. Final embroidery is slightly raised above the garment. You can create e.g. original t-shirt or a towel with monogram by using embroidery.
