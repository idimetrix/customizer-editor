Delivering excellent results, Flocking is among the top quality printing techniques available.

Flocking is the right choice if you would like to print a vector motif with up to a max. 3 colours, and without colour gradients. Flock Printing guarantees you a high wearing comfort.

Flocking is done by large colour plotters that mechanically cut your motif or text out of the flocking foil. The foil is then pressed onto the products surface under high pressure, and at a high temperature. Finally, the plastic foil is removed and your shirt is ready. Flocked shirts really feel like velvet on your skin.
