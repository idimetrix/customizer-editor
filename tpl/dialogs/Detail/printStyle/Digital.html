Digital transfer printing is the quickest, cheapest and most simple method for bringing designs with colour gradients onto white or very bright textiles in photo quality.

First, the design (e.g. a pixel motif) is printed onto the transfer plastic using a common colour laser printer. It is then pressed onto the T-Shirt at high temperature. Digital transfer is the right printing method for multi-colour designs, pixel motifs and designs with colour gradients.

Please note that there is no white colour available in digital transfer printing. This means that digital transfer printing doesn´t make any sense if you want to print onto coloured textiles, as the textile´s colour will shine through. Also, you should know that the colours of your digitally printed shirt will fade out if you wash it frequently. Shirts printed using the digital transfer technique will normally not last longer than 2 years.
